
# Phishing Awareness Training

This project is to educate people about Phishing attacks, types of phishing attacks, and best practices and tipes against phishing attacks as well as real-world examples. This presentation briefly describes phishing attacks, why it works or succeeds and also some social engineering tactics attackers use to trick users and as well how to recognize phishing attacks through emails.


## Used By

This project is used by the company:

- GAO TEK



## Lessons Learned

- Various types of phishing attacks and their areas of attack.
- Why phishing attacks work.
- Social engineering tactics and techniques used by attackers.
